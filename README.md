# PMZ Pricing Assistant

**Performance Margin Pricing Assistant** — Digital paper & pencil estimating with real LEM costing and margin tracking.

---

## The Philosophy

> **“Profit isn’t a number. It’s a culture.”**

Most contractors price from competitor guesses, market rates, or gut feel.  
The **PMZ Pricing Assistant** flips that model.

It helps contractors calculate what they **must** charge based on their own real costs — Labor, Equipment, and Material (LEM) — so every hour worked collects the money required to cover overhead, hit margin targets, and generate true net profit.

---

## The Four Pillars of Total Profit

| Pillar | Focus | Purpose |
|--------|-------|--------|
| **1. Overhead** | Clean P&L & true overhead cost | Know exactly what must be covered |
| **2. Work Type Targets** | Margin goals by job size & type | Set realistic, defendable targets |
| **3. LEM Costing** | True burdened costs | Labor + Equipment + Material at breakeven |
| **4. Bidding Discipline** | Protect the margin | Never leave money on the table |

---

## What It Does Right Now

- **Labor, Equipment & Material Rate Builders** — Build and save your true burdened rates once
- **Work Type Margin Targets** — Set different margin goals by job size and work type
- **Project Pricer** — Simple “digital paper & pencil” bid builder
- **Full Real LEM Breakdown** — See what a job actually costs you using your saved rates
- **Live Gross Profit Tracking** — See true margin vs target in real time

The goal is simple:  
Make it **so easy and intuitive** that contractors naturally start thinking in profit instead of just price.
